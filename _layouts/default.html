About This Project

New Gods: The Ancestral Mosaic is a vivid tribute and exploration into the artist’s identity — using ai tools to create & curate the merging of elements from personally relevant cultural mythologies in order to conceive a modern-day pantheon. Each piece is a deeply personal homage to the harmonious blending of ancestral traditions and the ever-evolving, complicated story of interconnected cultures across generations.
